About me

I'm Sandro, PhD Student working at CLIC Lab (CIMeC - University of Trento, Italy) under the supervision of Raffaella Bernardi (CIMeC, DISI). My current research is focused on the learning of quantified expressions (numbers and quantifiers), mass/count nouns grounding, and gradable adjectives from Language and Vision. For the behavioral experiments, I'm co-supervised by Manuela Piazza (CIMeC).

Before, I graduated in Linguistics (MSc) and Modern Literature (BSc) at the University of Padova (Italy). During the academic years, I've been working as a reporter for the newspaper Corriere del Veneto and as a book reviewer for the literary blog SulRomanzo.
