Sandro Pezzelle

I'm Sandro, PhD Student working at CLIC Lab (CIMeC - University of Trento, Italy) with the supervision of Raffaella Bernardi (CIMeC, DISI). My current research is focused on the learning of quantified expressions (numbers and quantifiers), mass/count noun distinction, and gradable adjectives from Language and Vision. The behavioral experiments are co-supervised by Manuela Piazza (CIMeC).

Before, I graduated in Linguistics (MSc) and Modern Literature (BSc) at the University of Padova (Italy).
