Sandro Pezzelle

About me

Assistant Professor in Responsible AI at ILLC, Faculty of Science of the University of Amsterdam. Affiliated with the Dialogue Modelling Group. Working on cognitively-inspired, multimodal models of language understanding and generation. As such, my research combines Natural Language Processing, AI, and Cognitive Science. My research has been published in ACL, EACL, EMNLP, NAACL, TACL, Cognition, and Cognitive Science.

Before, I was a Postdoc within the DREAM (Distributed dynamic REpresentations for diAlogue Management) ERC project led by Raquel Fernández.

In 2018, I obtained a PhD in Cognitive and Brain Sciences, track Language, Interaction and Computation, at CIMeC, University of Trento, under the supervision of Raffaella Bernardi (see QUANTIT-CLIC for some work done during the PhD). I was intern at SAP AI Research.
