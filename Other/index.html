Indievano

I'm co-founder of Indievano, project born in 2017 and aimed at bringing acoustic, secret concerts in private houses. Ingredients for Indievano: indie music, friends, your sofa. Our first concert has been delighted by Moplen and it took place in Padua (Italy) on 2017, Feb 25th.

New dates in Padova and Rovereto soon. Stay tuned!
